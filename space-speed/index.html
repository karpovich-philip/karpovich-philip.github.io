ABOUT

SPACE SPEED is a dangerous journey through the changing, colorful and full of surprises of the universe. You will have to hone your skills, accumulate experience and learn to improvise in order to survive. You will meet interesting characters. Know that despite flying skills, you will still perish. But do not worry! Death here is just the beginning of a big new adventure!

CONTROL

You control only the player spaceship!

Press the UP button to move the spaceship to up
Press the DOWN button to move the spaceship to down
Press the LEFT button to move the spaceship to left
Press the RIGHT button to move the spaceship to right
Press the SPACE button to fire

RULES

  • Your task is to score as many points as possible.
  • The speed of the game is gradually increasing.
  • You can move the ship around the entire playing field.
  • You have 5 lives.
  • If you encounter an enemy, an UFO or a meteorite, you lose one life.
  • If an enemy bullet hits you, you also lose one life.
  • For every hit you get points:
    • For a big meteorite 200 points.
    • For a small meteorite 300 points.
    • FOR THE UFO 500 points.
    • For the enemy 1000 points.
  • Each shot will cost you 100 points.

Good luck, warrior!
